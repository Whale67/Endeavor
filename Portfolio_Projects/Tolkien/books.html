The Books of Tolkien

J.R.R. Tolkien is best know for writing The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings trilogy. These four books have become some of the most beloved classics of all time. Nearly every modern day fairy tale or fantasy can find its roots in Tolkien's tales. What some are not aware of is that Tolkien actually wrote many books, some about Middle Earth and some having absolutely nothing to do with Middle Earth. Every book by Tolkien is worth reading.
