The Hobbit

The Hobbit was written by Tolkien with the intention of being a children's book. It introduces Bilbo Baggins, Gandalf the Grey, Elrond, Gollum and other characters that would take a part in the Lord of the Rings trilogy. The Hobbit also introduces the reader to Middle Earth, Elves, Dwarves,
orcs, Hobbits, etc.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

The Lord of the Rings is a trilogy. It begins 60 years after the The Hobbit ends. The first book The Fellowship of the Ring begins the journey of Frodo Baggins into a world of darkness and danger as he attempts to hide the One Ring from the Dark Lord Sauron.

The History of Middle Earth

Tolkien wrote many books about Middle Earth other than The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings. He described the creation of Middle Earth and its history through the First, Second and Third Age.
