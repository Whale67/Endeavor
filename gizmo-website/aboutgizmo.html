All About Gizmo

Welcome to All about Gizmo, the Best Guinea Pig in History. Of course that is my opinion because he is my guinea pig. However, he is a very sweet little guy.


Gizmo is 5 years old. He has white and auburn fur. Gizmo belongs to the breed of guinea pig called short-hair. There are many different breeds of guinea pig. For example, Crush (who you can find below along with Romeo and Aristotle) is a teddy bear guinea pig. Romeo is part Abyssinian and part Alpaca. If you would like a quick reference on guinea pig breeds check out the enclosed article from wikipedia.



When Gizmo was young he liked to run around alot. Now that he is more mature he enjoys lying around on his pillow and being petted. He has always enjoyed snacks. Some of his favorite snacks are apples, carrots, blueberries and lettuce.
